About Us

Our History

Founded in 2020, Our Cafe has been a beloved fixture in the heart of Amravati. From humble beginnings as a small, family-owned establishment, we have grown into a thriving community hub, serving up delicious coffee and wholesome fare to our loyal patrons.

Our Values

At Our Cafe, we are committed to providing more than just great food and drinks. We believe in creating a welcoming space where everyone feels at home, fostering connections, and nurturing a sense of belonging within our community. Quality, authenticity, and sustainability are at the core of everything we do, from sourcing the finest ingredients to supporting local artisans and producers.

Meet Our Owners

Our cafe is owned and operated by a passionate team of five remarkable women, each bringing their unique talents and expertise to the table:

  • Emily - Co-founder
  • Sophia - Chef & Culinary Director
  • Olivia - Creative Director & Interior Designer
  • Ava - Operations Manager
  • Isabella - Marketing & Communications

Together, we are dedicated to creating memorable experiences for our guests and making a positive impact in the communities we serve.
